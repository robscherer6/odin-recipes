Baked Salmon

Description:

Delicious baked salmon coated with Dijon-style mustard and seasoned bread crumbs, and topped with butter.

Ingredients:

  • 4 (4 ounce) fillets salmon
  • 3 tablespoons prepared Dijon-style mustard
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • ¼ cup Italian-style dry bread crumbs
  • ¼ cup butter, melted

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line a shallow baking pan with aluminum foil.
  2. Place salmon skin-side down on the prepared baking pan. Spread a thin layer of mustard on the top of each fillet, and season with salt and pepper. Top with bread crumbs, then drizzle with melted butter.
  3. Bake in a preheated oven until salmon flakes easily with a fork, about 15 minutes.
